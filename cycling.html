Prime Fitness cycling classes are designed as high intensity interval training rides, ridden to the beat with varying levels of resistance and tempos. Instructors are inspirational, music is motivational and the group energy is unparalleled.

Hop on a bike to experience indoor road Cycling on rides built to mimic the unpredictability of real-world terrain. Our take on traditional indoor Cycling, road classes bring the challenge of hills, intervals, and sprints indoors.

Ride Road training the Prime Fitness way. Power your performance with this results-driven ride that combines the unpredictability of real-world roads set to a driving and addictive playlist.

FACE THE PURSUIT

Get in the game and push your performance out of its comfort zone. Pursuit Burn with five stages of precisely timed high-intensity intervals, this class is a hardcore competitive Cycling experience scientifically designed to push you to a new level of fitness. The goal is to burn as much energy as you can, so just ride—and ride hard.

Pursuit Build focus on the power of the team and play your role in pushing past the finish line. Take on three intense and demanding hill stages designed to improve endurance and increase strength. The goal of the class is to travel a collective distance, so with every ride, you get stronger and work harder—for longer.

BENEFIT

Compared to outdoor cycling, riding indoor bike gives you more control over your speed, resistance, and overall power output. The result is a tough, low-impact workout that strengthens your core, glutes, hamstrings, quads, and more.

Cycling classes help you tune into your body as you try to match your movements with the music. You’re able to push your body further than you would on your own.
